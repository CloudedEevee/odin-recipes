Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Oatmeal raisin cookies hold a special place in my heart.

Even when cheaply made, they are still delicious. But why have store-bought, when you could make them so much better at home?

Simplicity is the game with this. Start by mixing the wet ingredients together. In a separate bowl, mix everything but the rolled oats and raisins. Mix the two bowls together, and then incorporate the oats and raisins.

From there, all you need to do is scoop a spoonful of the batter into dollops on your cookie sheet and bake!
